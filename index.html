National Park
THE great national park since 1890

Not just a great valley, but a shrine to human foresight, the strength
of granite, the power of glaciers, the persistence of life, and the tranquility of the High Sierra.


First protected in 1864, Yosemite National Park is best known for
its waterfalls, but within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find
deep valleys, grand meadows, ancient giant sequoias, a vast
wilderness area, and much more.
